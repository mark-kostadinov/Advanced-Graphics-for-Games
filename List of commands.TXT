- Pressing the Left and Right arrow keys cycle forward and backward through the scenes.
- Pressing the Pause key toggles whether the program will automatically cycle through the scenes.
- Pressing [1] will reset the weather in the first scene.
- Pressing [2] will toggle snow in the first scene.
- Pressing [3] will toggle rain in the first scene.
- Pressing [4] will toggle the shadow debug mode in every scene.
- Pressing [5] will toggle the moving light mode in every scene.

The commands available for each scene are listed in the top left corner of the screen, along with the framerate and GPU memory usage.
